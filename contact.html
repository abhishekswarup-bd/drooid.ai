Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with us

How quickly can you start? +
Most POCs kick off within a week of our initial call. We prioritize rapid discovery and validation, so you can see results fast. Our streamlined onboarding process means minimal setup friction—we get to building right away. Depending on your project complexity, we can have a working prototype or proof of concept ready for feedback within 2–4 weeks.
What does a typical POC cost? +
Our POCs are designed to be lean and focused. Pricing depends on scope, complexity, and timeline, but we typically work in fixed-price engagements to eliminate guesswork. We'll outline a clear proposal after our discovery call so you know exactly what to expect. We believe in transparency and delivering exceptional value for your investment.
Do we need to provide data? +
We work with whatever you have. If you have production data, great—we can dive straight in. If not, we'll help you generate synthetic or sample data to validate the approach. We're flexible and pragmatic. For sensitive data, we handle everything securely and can work within your compliance and privacy requirements. The goal is proving the concept works, regardless of data availability.
What happens after the POC? +
You own everything we build. The code, the models, the architecture—all yours. We provide comprehensive documentation and can help you transition the solution to your team or infrastructure. Many clients choose to continue with us for production build-out and ongoing support, but it's entirely your call. We're here to enable your success, not lock you in.
